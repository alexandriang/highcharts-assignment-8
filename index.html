Gun-Related Deaths in the United States

By Alexandria Ng

The rate of gun-related deaths in the United States fell throughout the 1990s. Measured by both gender, deaths steadily decreased each year. Measured by different age groups, deaths decreased as well. The charts below highlight these trends over time. Each chart records the number of deaths per 100,000 people each year.
